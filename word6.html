Word:Abomasum

    Definition: The fourth stomach of a ruminant, where the digestion of food occurs.

    Example Sentence: The abomasum is crucial for the digestion of proteins in cows.

  • The final chamber in the digestive system of ruminants.
  • It secretes digestive enzymes and acids to break down food.
