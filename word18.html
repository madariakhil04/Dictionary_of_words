Word:sanotoria

    Definition: A medical facility for the treatment of chronic diseases, especially tuberculosis.

    Example Sentence: The patient was sent to a sanatorium for long-term care and recovery.

  • A place providing care and treatment for patients with chronic illnesses.
  • Often located in a rural or mountainous area for fresh air and rest.
