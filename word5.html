Word: palliate

    Definition: To make a disease or its symptoms less severe without curing it.

    Example Sentence: The doctor prescribed medication to palliate the patient's pain.

  • To relieve or lessen without curing; to mitigate.
  • To disguise the seriousness of something by providing excuses or apologies.
synonyms antonyms
