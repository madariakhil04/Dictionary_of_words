Word:Technocracy

    Definition: A system of government or management where decision-makers are selected based on their technical expertise and knowledge.

    Example Sentence: The technocracy implemented policies that prioritized scientific research and technological innovation.

  • A form of governance by technical experts.
  • Emphasizes the role of technology in decision-making processes.
synonym antonym
