Word: Ferment

    Definition: A state of agitation or intense activity, often leading to change or development.

    Example Sentence: The political ferment in the country led to significant reforms.

  • A process of chemical breakdown, especially in the context of brewing or winemaking.
  • A state of unrest or excitement that can lead to change.
