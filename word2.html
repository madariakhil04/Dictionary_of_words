Word:Calibre

    Definition: the quality of someone's character or the level of their ability.

    Example Sentence: "The team was made up of players of the highest calibre.".

  • The diameter of the bore, as a cannon or other firearm or of any tube; or the weight of the projectile.
  • The degree of excellence or importance of a person or thing.
